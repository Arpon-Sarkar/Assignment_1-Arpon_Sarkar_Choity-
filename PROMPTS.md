# AI Prompts Used

## Placeholder Section (FAQ)

**Prompt:**
> Generate a "Frequently Asked Questions" section for the DevConf 2026 developer conference landing page. Come up with 4 unique, realistic questions attendees would ask before registering (venue/date, experience level, workshop details, refund policy). Write concise, on-brand answers in a professional tone. The section should visually match a clean, modern white/blue design system with a light gray background, an eyebrow label ("Before you register"), bold heading, and expandable question cards using native `<details>`/`<summary>`.

**Why FAQ:**
An FAQ section is a natural fit for a conference landing page — it removes friction before registration and matches the informational, trust-building tone of the rest of the site.
