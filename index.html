Before you register

Questions, answered

Everything attendees ask us in the weeks leading up to the conference.

Where and when is DevConf 2026 held?

DevConf 2026 takes place October 14–16 at Moscone West in San Francisco, with a hybrid livestream for remote attendees.

Do I need to be an experienced engineer to attend?

Not at all. Tracks are labeled by depth — from intro sessions for career switchers to deep architectural talks for staff engineers.

What's included in the workshop sessions?

Every workshop is capped at 40 seats, includes prepared cloud sandboxes, and is led by a maintainer or core contributor of the technology covered.

Can I get a refund if my plans change?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Within 30 days, your ticket becomes transferable to a colleague at no cost.
